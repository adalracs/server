The blockchain company

Our blockchain is designed to meet the needs of developers, tech startups, companies, non-governmental organizations, academic institutions, and governments, all of whom seek transparency, data sovereignty, and security. Adsum-Blockchain offers an accessible and flexible platform that, in addition to being low-cost, provides tools to create decentralized applications (DApps). Our solution enables the development of innovative projects involving decentralized identity, transparency, and authenticity.

Our mission is to democratize access to blockchain technology by providing an accessible and efficient platform for creating decentralized applications (DApps). We enable any technology enthusiast to materialize their ideas and projects, promoting data sovereignty and transparency at all levels. We are committed to ensuring that end users have full control over their data and transactions, fostering an environment of trust and security.

Things I Can Do

  • PoA Consensus Algorithm: High efficiency and speed.
  • Enhanced Security: Only authorized nodes can validate transactions.
  • Scalable Platform: High processing capacity and fast transactions.
  • Free microservices and APIs for developers.

We offer an accessible blockchain infrastructure, allowing developers and technology enthusiasts of all levels to participate and create solutions that promote data sovereignty, ensuring that end users have full control over their data and transactions. We foster a collaborative environment where developers, companies, academic institutions, and organizations can work together, share knowledge, and collectively advance blockchain technology.

A Few Accomplishments

  • Trust: Users should feel that they can fully trust the applications that use our platform.
  • Innovation: We want to inspire a sense of innovation, creativity, and transparency with robust mechanisms that ensure integrity and privacy.
  • Community: Build an active and supportive community where knowledge and experiences can be shared, working together towards common goals.

This is achieved through:

  • Successful implementation of nodes by multiple organizations.
  • Development of decentralized applications in various industries.
  • Growth of an active developer community.
